About Me

I am a second-year graduate student in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park advised by Professor Tom Goldstein.

I am broadly interested in the intersection of deep learning, computer vision, and graphics. My recent work focuses on 3D neural scene reconstruction and multimodal biometric recognition.

I am currently funded by the IARPA Walk-through Rendering from Images of Varying Altitude (WRIVA) program.

PUP 3D-GS: Principled Uncertainty Pruning for 3D Gaussian Splatting
Preprint, 2024.
PAPER CODE

Prune 88.44% of primitives from 3D Gaussiance Splatting (3D-GS) scenes using a mathematically principled sensitivity pruning score, tripling rendering speed with minimal loss of visual fidelity.

Alex Hanson*, Allen Tu*, Vasu Singla, Mayuka Jayawardhana, Matthias Zwicker, Tom Goldstein.
